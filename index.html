About Us

GoodEarth GEWA App

The GoodEarth GEWA app is our official resident management platform, available on Android. It allows residents of our communities to:

  • Raise and track maintenance requests
  • Monitor utility usage (water, electricity, gas)
  • Manage visitor access
  • Control smart home features
  • Receive community announcements

Privacy Policy

GoodEarth GEWA App
Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

GoodEarth GEWA is operated by GAIA Properties and Infrastructure India Private Limited. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

  • Name, email address, and phone number — collected during registration for account creation and identity verification
  • Property unit number — to associate your account with your residence
  • OTP/verification codes — sent via SMS or email for secure login, not stored after verification
  • Payment information — processed securely through Razorpay; we do not store card details on our servers
  • Device push notification token — to send community announcements and alerts

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your resident account
  • To send OTP verification codes for login
  • To process utility payments (gas, water)
  • To send community announcements and maintenance updates
  • To manage visitor passes and vehicle records

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your data may be shared with:

  • Razorpay — for payment processing
  • Twilio — for OTP SMS delivery
  • Good Earth management — for community administration

5. Data Security

All data is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. We use industry-standard security practices to protect your information.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time from Settings → Profile → Delete Account.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.
